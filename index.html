ABOUT INDY PERK

ERICA HERNANDEZ — Owner/Chef/Coffeeist

Indy Perk was established in 2017 by Erica Hernandez, a Chicago native with many years of experience in the restaurant business. The cafe was inspired by her previous venture, Indy Cafe, located in East Chicago, Indiana, where all the food items were made in-house from scratch.

We serve house brewed coffee, hand crafted lattes, and frappes. In addition to our drinks, we serve fresh, made-to-order soups, salads and sandwiches, as well as a selection of gourmet muffins, cookies and other goodies.
